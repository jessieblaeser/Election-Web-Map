Vizualizing the 2020 Presidential Election Two Different Ways

Taking inspiration from The New York Times Upshot's own introspection of the many ways to vizualize election results, I have tried two tactics below: a choropleth map and a graduated cylinder map.

Both maps are ✨interactive✨ (go ahead, click and zoom around!) and were made using MapBox, geopandas and javascript.

Just two counties in Kentucky voted for Joe Biden in 2020

Zoom in and out to see state and county results across the country. Click on each state or county for more detailed information about each candidate's performance.
